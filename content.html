About iTriage Solutions for Rural/Community Hospitals

Community and rural hospitals face unique challenges including limited resources, low population density, and a higher percentage of patients with chronic conditions. Improving care outcomes and the overall health of your community starts with engaging and connecting with residents when they are searching for information and access to care.

iTriage gives consumers an easy and convenient way to take charge of their healthcare by navigating their symptoms, conditions, procedures and medications. Step-by-step mobile and online tools help them make better decisions about how to effectively and affordably find medical treatment, work with doctors, and manage their health.

A quarter of the U.S. population live in rural areas, and in those smaller communities, hospitals are often the primary source of information about health issues, resources and medical care. Learn more about how to:

  • Improve health literacy

    Navigating the healthcare system can be confusing. Help your patients understand the tests and medications they are being prescribed. Give them easy access to appointment scheduling and information about managing chronic conditions.

  • Maximize patient engagement

    Connect with patients when they have an immediate medical need and are seeking information and care.

  • Reduce out-migration

    Make it easy for patients to find you, learn about your specialties, make appointments, stay in your network and interact with your providers.

  • Steer patients to appropriate care options

    Help your community make better healthcare decisions by steering them to the most appropriate level of care for their condition.

  • Increase patient satisfaction

    Improve patient satisfaction scores by providing a better healthcare experience for patients.

  • Improve care coordination

    Early check-in, mobile and online appointment setting, and appointment setting at ED discharge simplify access at connection points.
